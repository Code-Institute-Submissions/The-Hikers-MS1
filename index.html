About

Meet our team

Welcome to the Ireland hiking of our dreams, we hope yours too

If you love hiking, you are fascinated by wonderful views, looking for adventure and you would like to know more about the interesting history about Ireland, then you have come to the right place

We are a small group of friends who are passionate about hiking in Ireland. We have walked the Irish mountains for many kilometers and each time it is an amazing and unforgettable experience for us.

Our certified guide Arek and the main organizer who is passionate about the history of Ireland, can tell you many interesting stories about this beautiful, scenic and wonderful country.

We hope to see you soon on one of our trips

Tour option to chose

We are organizing hiking tours from half day up to three days including a certified tour guide with a high sense of humor and include different variations for the trails in the region you choose to go to, depending on your time, desire, age and level of advancement, we will select and organize a trip for you. The price is estimated depending on the number of days you choose, season, accommodation and food you choose. Contact us for more information. Tell us what you expect and we will organize a trip for you.
